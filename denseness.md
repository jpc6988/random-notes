# Denseness of $\\{m+\sqrt2n\mid n\in\mathbb N\\}$ in $\mathbb R$

Prove that the set $S\coloneqq\\{m+\sqrt2n\mid n\in\mathbb N\\}\subset\mathbb R$ is dense in $\mathbb R$.

*Proof:* Let $r=\sqrt 2-1$. Assume that $r\in(0,1)$ (without proof).
